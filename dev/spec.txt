# The FRAME spec

## Visual
- Resolution: 64x64
- Colors: 8

## Architecture
- Memory: 16-bit (0x0000-0xFFFF)
- Registers: 4

## Memory Mapping
- 0x0000-0xEFFF: program memory

## Instruction Set
- MOV [v], [r]: Moves value [v] into [r]

## Instruction Formats

### O
15 14 13 12 11 10 09 08|07 06 05|04 03 02 01 00
-----------------------|mode    |opcode

### A
15 14 13 12 11|10 09 08|07 06 05|04 03 02 01 00
--------------|reg. A  |mode    |opcode

### K
15 14|13 12 11 10 09 08|07 06 05|04 03 02 01 00
constant               |mode    |opcode

### AB
15 14|13 12 11|10 09 08|07 06 05|04 03 02 01 00
-----|reg. B  |reg. A  |mode    |opcode

### ABC
16 15 14|13 12 11|10 09 08|07 06 05|04 03 02 01 00
reg. C  |reg. B  |reg. A  |mode    |opcode

### ABK
21 20 19 18 17 16 15 14|13 12 11|10 09 08|07 06 05|04 03 02 01 00
constant               |reg. B  |reg. A  |mode    |opcode

## Assembler
- Instructions: mov, add, sub, etc.
- Labels: @label
- Numbers: 1, -6, 0xf3, 0b0110, 0o777, etc.
- Identifiers: my_Cool_Identifier_3, r0, etc.
